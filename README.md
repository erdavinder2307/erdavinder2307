## About Me

I'm the founder of **[Solidev Electrosoft](https://www.solidevelectrosoft.com)** — a software product studio building AI-powered applications and enterprise systems from Chandigarh, India.

Over 13+ years I've shipped production software for **LexisNexis**, **Fairway Independent Mortgage Corporation**, and SaaS startups across 4 continents. I've personally designed and launched **8 live software products** across healthcare, fintech, productivity, and social platforms.

I work as an engineering partner for startups and businesses that need:
- A **technical co-founder** who understands product and business, not just code
- A **senior AI engineer** to integrate GPT-4o, Claude, or Gemini into their product
- A **full stack architect** to design and ship scalable cloud-native systems
- A **mobile specialist** who delivers Flutter + native iOS/Android apps to production
